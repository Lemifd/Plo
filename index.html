Our Program

Discover our programs at the PLO LUMUMBA FOUNDATION Ethiopian Chapter, driving education, empowerment, and development in Ethiopia.

Book Donations

By donating books to rural schools, we aim to enhance educational resources, foster a love for reading, and facilitate the sharing of knowledge among students and community members.

Mentorship Programme

Programme aimed at inculcating African principles, values and cultures that impart positive change for the transformation of the African Youth across the African continent

Youth Leadership Development

Providing opportunities for youth leadership training, mentorship, and capacity-building to cultivate the next generation of leaders.

Community Health Education

Educating communities on health and hygiene practices, disease prevention, and promoting overall well-being through informative workshops and campaigns

Environmental Conservation Campaigns

Undertaking initiatives to raise awareness about environmental conservation, sustainable practices, and biodiversity protection.

Advocacy and Conflict Resolution

Advocating for social issues and promoting peaceful conflict resolution strategies to build harmonious and resilient communities.
